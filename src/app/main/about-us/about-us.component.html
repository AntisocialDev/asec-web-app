ABOUT US

Alfred Susan Event Centre (ASEC) is a premier event venue located in the heart of Ijebu-ode, Ogun State. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional event spaces and unforgettable experiences for our clients and their guests.

OUR STORY

ASEC was founded with a vision to create a world-class event center that would serve as the go-to destination for memorable events in Ijebu-ode and its surroundings. Named after our CEO's parents, Alfred and Susan, our center embodies a legacy of hospitality and excellence.

Over the years, we have hosted countless weddings, corporate events, concerts, festivals, and private parties, earning a reputation for professionalism, reliability, and exceptional service.
