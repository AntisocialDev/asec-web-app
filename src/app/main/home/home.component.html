WELCOME TO ASEC

Alfred Susan Event centre (ASEC) is located in the eastern part of Ogun state in a city called Ijebu-ode. It is only 110km by road north-east of Lagos. It is home to some of the biggest and best events and festivals in Ijebu. Our large indoor capacity and parking space makes it suitable for corporate events, special events and festivals. You also have resturants, bars and hotels just steps away. Want to make your next event fun and memorable? Click here to contact us about your next event!
